# Daily Update — June 26, 2026

*Written for non-technical stakeholders. Plain language, no jargon.*

## In one line

Staff can now **post notes directly from the dashboard**, those notes appear instantly in Monday.com under their own name, and appointment cards now show the **time** of each consult.

---

## Consult times now visible on appointment cards

Previously, the Appointments page showed the date of each consult but not the time. The time was already stored in Monday.com but was being discarded when the data was pulled into the local database.

That's fixed. Both the compact board-view cards and the full list-view cards now show the consult time next to the date (for example, "Jun 26 · 10:30 AM"). No changes were needed in Monday.com — the time was always there, the dashboard just wasn't displaying it.

---

## Post notes from the dashboard — they appear in Monday.com

A note composer box has been added in two places:

1. **Client profile pages** — in the Overview tab, above the existing timeline of updates.
2. **Appointment focus panel** — the hover/click panel that appears when you focus on an appointment card.

Type a note and press the "Post Note" button (or ⌘↵ on a Mac). The note is:
- Sent to Monday.com immediately and appears in that client's Monday.com item under Updates.
- Added to the dashboard timeline right away — no refresh needed.

---

## Notes now posted under each person's own Monday.com name

When notes were posted before, they always appeared in Monday.com as coming from the shared firm account. That's no longer the case.

Each person on the team can now connect their personal Monday.com account to the dashboard. Once connected, any note they post shows up in Monday.com under their name — just as if they had typed it directly in Monday.com.

### How to connect your account

1. Go to **Settings** (the gear icon in the sidebar).
2. Find the **Monday.com Account** section near the top.
3. Click **Connect Monday.com** and follow the one-time sign-in prompt.
4. Done — your name will appear on all notes you post going forward.

This only needs to be done once per person, per browser. The connection is remembered.

### Reminder banner

If you open the note composer without having connected your Monday.com account, a small amber notice appears:

> *Hey! Your Monday.com account isn't connected — notes will post under the firm's shared account. **Connect your account →***

Clicking the link takes you directly to Settings. Notes can still be posted without connecting — they just won't show your name in Monday.com.

---

## Direct link to Monday.com from any client profile

A small **"Monday.com ↗"** link now appears in the top bar of every client's profile page. Clicking it opens that client's Monday.com item in a new tab — no searching required.
