Each client's dashboard is getting a single, complete history — not just the notes we already had, but every email, phone call, and logged activity from Monday.com, woven into one timeline you can filter. ---

## The big one: a unified client timeline

Until now, a client's page showed the **notes and comments** we sync from Monday.com. But a huge part of each case's real story lives somewhere else in Monday.com — the **"Emails & Activities"** log: the actual emails sent and received, phone-call notes, text-message logs, and the custom activity entries the team creates (Consult notes, Contract notes, Hearing notes, and so on).

Those two histories were separate. Now they're becoming **one**.

Every client's timeline will show, in a single chronological stream:

- **Notes and comments** (what we already had), plus
- **Emails** — with the subject line and who sent them,
- **Phone calls and text logs**,
- **Activities** — labeled with the team's own categories (Consult note, Contract note, RingCentral SMS, etc.).

Everything is attributed to the **right staff member** and sorted by date, so opening a client shows the full picture at a glance. You can also **filter** the timeline — show only emails, only activities, only notes — or see everything together.

---

## Why this matters for the team

Instead of hopping between Monday.com screens to reconstruct what's happened on a case, the dashboard becomes the **single place to catch up on a client**: the last email that went out, the call a paralegal logged, the note the attorney left — all in one view, in order. It's the difference between a partial record and the complete story of each case.

## We confirmed it works with your real data

Before building anything, we verified against the live system that this information is actually reachable for your boards — and it is. We then ran a small, safe test import that pulled **real emails and activities** for a sample of clients (interview-scheduling emails, call notes, contract follow-ups) and confirmed they landed correctly, in the right order, under the right people, with duplicates automatically removed.

The software was also put through the usual automated safety checks, all of which pass.

---

## A note on duplicates

The same email or activity can appear on more than one connected record for a client. The system now **detects and removes those duplicates automatically**, so the timeline shows each real event once — no clutter, no double-counting.

---

## The large data import (running now)

A one-time import is currently running to populate every client's timeline with their full email-and-activity history. It's a big job — this history turns out to be **even richer than our existing notes**, with tens of thousands of entries already imported and more coming. Monday.com was responding slowly earlier in the day, which stretched the import out, but it's progressing steadily and safely.

Client data was protected throughout: a verified backup was taken before the import, and the client database is rebuilt from Monday.com itself, so nothing depends on a single copy.
